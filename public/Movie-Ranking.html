Best Ever Movies That Changed My Life

Top 3 Movies

1. Pursuit of HappYness

It is a great movie in terms of your motivation and not giving up in challenging situations.

It is highly recommended to watch this movie if you want to solidify your go-getter spirit like "you can do it!"

2. Life of Pi

Life of Pi is a super insprational movie that makes you think deep about what is life and how the individual lives gather and harmonize in our society.

Besides the meaningful core message of the movie, I personally loved the look and feel of the film color patterns. This beautifully shot movie can refreh your feeling with the beautiful scene in the emerald ocean.

3. Steven Jobs

Are you an aspiring startup enterpreneur? If you are, this is a must-watch movie!

The film portrays the journey of Steve Jobs' life, showcasing his innovative contributions to Apple from its origins to its ultimate conclusion.

You might become a super aspiring entrepreneur and aspired to create a new value to the world by the end of the movie. :)
